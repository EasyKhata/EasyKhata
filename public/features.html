EasyKhata Features

EasyKhata is a ledger and record management app for apartment societies, small businesses, freelancers, and households in India.

Website: www.easykhata.net

Open App Terms Privacy

Apartment Society Management

  • Track flat-wise monthly maintenance dues and collections.
  • Set and apply maintenance amount to all flats.
  • Record society expenses and download resident-shareable reports.
  • Send payment reminders and receipts over WhatsApp.

Small Business and Freelancer Workflows

  • Record income and expenses with monthly or yearly views.
  • Create invoices and track paid, pending, and overdue status.
  • Manage clients, services, and collection history in one place.

Household Ledger

  • Track household income, spending, and EMI commitments.
  • Get clear summaries for month-wise cashflow decisions.

Reports and Export

  • Download PDF reports for monthly and financial-year summaries.
  • Generate share-ready statements for apartment residents and teams.
